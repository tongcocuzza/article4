Vanilla Cupcakes

I love baking when I have a free time but I am not a profession. So, I get most of great recipes from watching YouTube channels. I modified this cupcake recipe from Gracious Treatz Channel and added my cupcakes with some chocolate chunks which I like Nestle semi-sweet chocolate chunks.

Ingredients

  • 90g granulated sugar
  • 220g plain yogurt
  • 80g vegetable oil
  • 150g cake flour
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 36pcs semi-sweet chocolate chunks
  1. preheat oven to 350 F and prepare 6 jumbo cupcake liners.
  2. add sugar and yogurt in a large bowl and mix until the sugar is dissolved.
  3. add oil and vanilla extract and mix until they are well incorporated
  4. sift all the dry ingredients. Fold in flour to the yogurt mixture until the flour disappear.
  5. scoop the mixture into cupcake liners and add chocolate chunks.
  6. bake them in a preheated oven at 350 F for about 15-17 minutes.
  7. cool them before frosting them with buttercream.

Maple Buttercream

  • 4oz. (1stick) unsalted butter, softened
  • 45g confectioners’ sugar
  • 85g maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/8 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  1. whip the softened butter until light and fluffy.
  2. sift the confectioners’ sugar then add to the butter, mix until well combined.
  3. add maple syrup, vanilla extract, cinnamon and salt, whip until smooth and creamy.
